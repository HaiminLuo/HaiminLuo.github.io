Biography

Master student in Visual and Data Intelligence Center(VDI), School of Information Science and Technology(SIST), ShanghaiTech University.

I am a third-year Master student in Computer Science at ShanghaiTech University, working with Prof. Jingyi Yu. I also work closely with Prof. Lan Xu. I received my bachelor’s degree in 2019 from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

My research interest lies in Neural Modeling and Rendering, 3D Reconstruction, Motion Capture and Animation.
